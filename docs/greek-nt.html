Optimized Reading Order Analysis

Both strategies start with Luke, which covers 2,038 unique lemmas (38.1% of the total Greek NT vocabulary) and has the highest overlap with the most frequent words across the corpus.

Key insight: By reading Luke first, you learn vocabulary that recurs throughout the entire Greek NT — giving you maximum return on your study investment from day one.
