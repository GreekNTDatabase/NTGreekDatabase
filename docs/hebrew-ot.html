Optimized Reading Order Analysis

Both strategies start with Isaiah, which covers 2,391 unique lemmas (28.5% of the total Hebrew OT vocabulary) and has the highest overlap with the most frequent words across the corpus.

Key insight: By reading Isaiah first, you learn vocabulary that recurs throughout the entire Hebrew OT — giving you maximum return on your study investment from day one.
